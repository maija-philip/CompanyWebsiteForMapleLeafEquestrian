Maple Leaf Equestrian

An amazing place to learn horsemanship and ride no matter how much previous experience. We'd love to help you further your riding skills or get you started riding horses for the first time!

Who are we?

Welcome to Maple Leaf Equestrian, we are a small barn out of Pagemill Pastures. Every member of our team loves horses and will work to help you achieve your goals. Wether those goals are to trot for the first time or successfully complete a full course, we guarantee you’ll be able to accomplish them in a safe, friendly and fun environment.

Lessons and Camp

Here at Maple Leaf Equestrian, we offer adult and child horseback riding lessons. Each lesson includes horsemanship, like getting the horse ready for riding, as well as on the horse instruction. Everyone is welcome for lessons and it’s a great way to get active. It’s also shown to be super helpful for people with disabilities, learning to connect with an animal and building confidence.
