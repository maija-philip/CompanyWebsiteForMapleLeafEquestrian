About Us

We are a lesson barn inside of Pagemill Pastures, which is a really prime location right off Pagemill. It’s super pretty and peaceful and we’d love for you to come see it. At our barn we teach not only on the horse, but before and after we ride as well. We want to teach you how to take care of horses and ride for a well rounded and fun experience.
