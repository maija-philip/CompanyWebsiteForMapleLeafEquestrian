Our Horses

We’d like to introduce you to all of our horses. They are all rescue horses that we’ve rehabbed to make great lesson horses

Malibu

Malibu is up for anything. She’ll teach you a lot each time you ride her. Her three most favorite things in life are rolling, carrots, and grass. She’s definitely got a wonderful personality and would love to meet you.

Jasmine

Jasmine’s the newest member of our family, she was originally owned by a girl with cerebral palsy who absolutely adored her. They had an amazing connection and were able to do so much. She’s now part of our lesson program and everyone loves her so much!

Haji

Haji is one of our energetic ponies. He’s always ready to go and have fun. We were shocked when we learned that he was 14 years old, which is middle aged for a horse, because acts so young. :) He is a full Arabian and he’s super happy and cute all the time.

Paco

Paco is the most senior of our horses, but he’s still going strong. He’s super sweet and will take care of you very well. He’s definitely earned the title schoolmaster.
