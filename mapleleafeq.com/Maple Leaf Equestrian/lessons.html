Lessons

We offer lessons for children and adults. In our lessons, we teach how to care for a horse as well as how to ride. And we welcome everyone.

Info about our Lessons

Our lessons are 30 minutes for private lessons, and can go up to an hour for group lessons. Semi-private lessons are usually somewhere in the middle of that timeframe. We also teach how to get the horse ready and care after riding.

Leasing a Pony

We offer a half leases on each horse. A lease is when you pay to ride just one specific horse of your choice. This way you can focus on getting better at a specific talent and develop a connection to the horse. Contact us for more information about leasing.
